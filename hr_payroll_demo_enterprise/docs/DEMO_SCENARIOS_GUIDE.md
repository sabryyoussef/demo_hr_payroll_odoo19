## Presentation Tip

Do not present this as "features".

Present it as a payroll operations center:

- HR owns master data and contracts.
- Attendance drives exceptions.
- Leaves drive paid/unpaid work entries.
- Payroll creates payslips.
- Finance sends salary transfers.
- Accounting reconciles and reviews exceptions.
- Managers use dashboards and mass actions to reduce manual work.
