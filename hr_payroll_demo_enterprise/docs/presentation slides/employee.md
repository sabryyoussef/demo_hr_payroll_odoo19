# Employees Module

The Employees module serves as the central HR operational hub within Odoo 19, providing a unified workspace for managing employee information, organizational structure, contracts, skills, onboarding processes, and workforce operations.

---

# Main Navigation Menus

## Employees

Centralized employee database including:

* Employee profiles
* Contact information
* Job positions
* Contract details
* Department assignment
* Work schedules
* Skills and certifications
* Employment history

---

## Departments

Organize the company structure by:

* Departments
* Teams
* Reporting hierarchy
* Department managers
* Workforce distribution

Supports multi-department and multi-company environments.

---

## Learning

Integrated employee development features including:

* Employee training
* Skills development
* Certifications
* Internal knowledge management
* Learning progress tracking

---

## Reporting

Real-time HR analytics and operational insights:

* Headcount analysis
* Department distribution
* Employee turnover
* Workforce allocation
* Attendance analytics
* HR KPI dashboards
